Hurricane Season


Arizona's own Hurricane Season is one of the HOTTEST Hip Hop duo in the valley! "Calm Before the Storm" is their highly anticipated upcoming project! Stay tuned and listen to their latest single "We Ride Slow"

How the Storm Began

Hurricane Season is united by Rain and Typhoon. Both artists living out of the state of Arizona started their Hip Hop career fairly recently. Being grade school friends Rain and Typhoon discovered their mutual passion in writing lyrics thru back and forth "text battles". Shortly after "text battles" turned into verses, which turned into songs. Together they began to create music they both enjoyed. With each individual having a completely different approach than the other they created a unique cohesion with their songs. Teaming up with Deep Roots Studios, Hurricane Season has taken their music to the next level making thier music commercial ready!

Even being new to the music scene Hurricane Season shows great pronmise to not only be an Arizona favorite, but a household name sometime very soon!
